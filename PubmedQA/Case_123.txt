Additionally, the sustained improvement in tinnitus was observed in only a subset of patients at follow-up. The authors themselves note the need for larger studies with more responsive outcome measures to confirm these findings.

**3. Conclusion**

The study provides preliminary evidence that multi-modal cervical physical therapy may improve tinnitus in patients with CST, as indicated by significant reductions in TFI and NBQ scores and a majority of patients reporting substantial improvement immediately after treatment. However, the small sample size and limited sustained improvement in tinnitus at follow-up suggest that the evidence is not yet robust.
