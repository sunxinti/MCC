- **Pale Stools**: Reflect **bile duct obstruction**, as unconjugated bilirubin cannot be excreted into the intestines, leading to pale/grey stools.  
- **Hepatomegaly**: Suggests liver inflammation or structural abnormalities (e.g., biliary obstruction).
